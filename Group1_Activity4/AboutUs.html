About
Us

Deo Gracias is a contemporary Spanish restaurant in Quezon City. Inspired by Spain's culinary heritage, our menu combines traditional recipes with a modern twist. Our name, Deo Gracias, derived from the Latin phrase "deo gratias" meaning "thanks [be] to God,” – an expression of gratitude for the tastes and flavors that Spain has to offer. Beyond the cuisine, our focus extends to creating an atmosphere that embodies the spirit of Spanish culture. With a blend of ambiance and warm hospitality, our restaurant provides the ideal setting to savor and enjoy la buena vida (the good life).

History

When founded their real Spanish restaurant, Deo Gracias, Cong. Deogracias Victor Savellano and Chef Heny Sison had big plans it. When they were first launched in October 2021 at 12 11th Jamboree St., QC, there was still uncertainty about the kind of response they would receive. The home where Deogracias is located has been transformed into a restaurant. With outside tables and a second story that can be used as function spaces or extra seating, the place has a warm atmosphere.

The restaurant actually closed for two weeks at the beginning of the year, when the Omicron health problem was at its worst. Fortunately, they resumed their course.
